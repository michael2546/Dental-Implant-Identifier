IMPLANTMIND

Your go-to tool for dental implant identification!

Get Started
Implant Mind is a tool that uses deep learning algorithms to identify the brand of a dental implant from a patient's X-ray image, immediate and in just few seconds.

With the power of Artificial Intelligence and other technologies it will help dentists and oral surgeons to save time, money, and avoid potential mistakes during the implant identification process.

Make it easy and Store your patients data like Implant Type , Diagnoses and more, we use One and linked database to register the patient data with a unique id to finally get rid of the problem of unidentified implants.
